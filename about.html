About the Röhrig Institut

Advancing well-being through research, innovation, and communication.

Aims and Objectives

Röhrig Institut GmbH is dedicated to enhancing quality of life through innovative, non-invasive, and drug-free ADJUVANT therapies.

Our primary aim is to advance healthcare by providing comprehensive training for professionals, uniquely integrating German language and cultural training to foster effective communication. Through these initiatives, we strive to make advanced therapies more accessible and beneficial for everyone.

We also proudly collaborate with Srinivas Medical University in Mangalore, India, on Indo-German projects focused on navigating pregnancy intolerances. This partnership integrates cutting-edge research with practical applications to develop innovative strategies that enhance maternal health and well-being globally.

Our History

Established as AAH Natürlich UG on September 25, 2020, the Röhrig Institute advances well-being through research and innovation. Founded by Prof. Dr. Amina Ather, now Prof. Dr. Afü Röhrig, the institute follows PPPM principles.

On June 8, 2024, the company transformed into a GmbH, introducing Abdul Majeed Kaamran with further responsibilities. Dr. med. Christopher Röhrig joined as Honorary Technical Advisor. AAH Natürlich GmbH is rebranding to Röhrig Institut GmbH (name change pending with notary).
