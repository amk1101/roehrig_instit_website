About Us

Röhrig Institut — Advancing Quality of Life

We focus on innovative, non-invasive, and drug-free adjuvant therapies. Our clinician-ready training uniquely integrates German language and cultural fluency to improve communication and care. We also collaborate with Srinivas Medical University (Mangalore) on Indo–German projects navigating pregnancy intolerances—turning research into better maternal health.

Our Project

RIDM — Research · Innovation · Development · Marketing

Initiated by Dr. Vincezo Costigliola and Prof. em. (PRC) Dr. med. (Ind) Afü Röhrig, with technical guidance from Dr. med. (Ger) Christopher Röhrig, RIDM advances integrated medicine by selectively incorporating complementary modalities into comprehensive plans alongside innovative diagnostics and therapies.

As precision health evolves, decisions and interventions are tailored to individual risk and response. RIDM translates this into clinician-ready training and patient-ready programs, emphasizing preventive, non-invasive, non-drug therapies that elevate everyday health.
