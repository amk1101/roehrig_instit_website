RIDM (Research Innovation Development Marketing) and training for quality of life.

Research innovation development and marketing (RIDM) is the project initiated by Dr. Vincezo Costigliola and Prof. em. (PRC) Dr. med. (Ind) Afü Röhrig, and technically guided by Dr. med. (Ger) Christopher Röhrig with focus on Research innovation development and marketing on Integrated medicine which is practicing medicine in a way that selectively incorporates elements of complementary and alternative medicine into comprehensive treatment plans alongside latest innovative methods of diagnosis and treatment for better quality of life.

With the evolving scientific innovations in personalized medicine, also referred to as precision medicine, is a medical model that separates people into different groups with medical decisions, practices, interventions and/or products being tailored to the individual patient based on their predicted response or risk of disease. The terms personalized medicine, precision medicine, stratified medicine and P4 medicine are used interchangeably to describe this concept though some authors and organizations use these expressions separately to indicate particular nuances.

While the tailoring of treatment to patients dates back at least to the time of Hippocrates, the term has risen in usage in recent years given the growth of new diagnostic and informatics approaches that provide an understanding of the molecular basis of disease, particularly genomics. This provides a clear evidence base on which to stratify (group) related patients. Personalized medicine has been identified as a key and prospective approach to “achieve optimal individual health decisions”, therefore overcoming the challenge to “Engineer better medicines“. The EMA team begins by connecting patients with preventive healthcare and personalized medicine using non-invasive and non-drug therapies to improve quality of life and achieve healthier living.

About the Röhrig Institut

Röhrig Institut GmbH advances quality of life with innovative, non-invasive, drug-free adjunct therapies. We equip healthcare professionals with up-to-date training that integrates German language and cultural fluency to improve patient outcomes. Through Indo-German collaboration with Srinivas Medical University (Mangalore), we address pregnancy intolerances, turning research into practical prenatal care.
