Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our pilot program.

Is the pilot program really free?

Yes, the current pilot is completely free of charge. We are offering this to gather feedback and refine our curriculum. Please note that seats are limited.

Who teaches the courses?

The lead instructor is Dr. Christopher Röhrig, a native German doctor. This ensures a high standard of language proficiency combined with a deep understanding of the German cultural and professional context, especially in healthcare.

Do you provide visa services?

No, our focus is exclusively on language and cultural training. We do not offer assistance with visa applications, accommodation, or job placement.

How do I join a course?

Simply fill out the registration form on our website. Once we receive your application, we will review it and send you an invitation and further details if a seat is available.
